Quick Answers

Where in Utah do you operate?

Everywhere. We're based along the Wasatch Front and serve Salt Lake City, Provo, Ogden, Park City, St. George, and everywhere in between. Travel is factored into your quote.

What do you need from my venue?

Not much: a dedicated standard power outlet and roughly 6' × 6' of solid, covered ground per simulator. Indoors or under cover outdoors both work. We handle the rest.

How much does it cost?

Every event is different — it depends on date, location, number of simulators, and hours. Send the form and we'll reply with a straightforward quote, usually within one business day.

How far in advance should I book?

As early as you can — we're launching in Utah with a limited number of dates, and weekends go first. That said, if your event is soon, ask anyway; we'll tell you honestly what's possible.
